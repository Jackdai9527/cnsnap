# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Cross-border shoppers who want to buy from Chinese marketplaces on mobile and desktop. They often discover products on the go, compare options quickly, and return later to finish payment, consolidation, or shipping tasks.

## Product Purpose
CNSnap helps users search, buy, inspect, consolidate, and ship products from China through one workflow. The current goal is to keep the desktop storefront stable while turning the mobile web experience into a more app-like shell that can later translate cleanly into packaged Android and iOS apps without losing crawlable content.

## Brand Personality
Reliable, guided, efficient.

## Anti-references
Do not make mobile feel like a shrunk desktop website. Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, over-glassy neon app chrome, or SEO-hostile interfaces that hide important content behind JS-only interactions.

## Design Principles
Keep desktop trust signals intact while giving mobile its own native-feeling layout.
Preserve the full content model on mobile so SEO and user comprehension stay aligned.
Make high-frequency actions reachable with one thumb and clear visual hierarchy.
Use mobile surfaces to reduce scanning effort, not to reduce information depth.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain readable text sizing on phones, strong contrast, visible focus states, and touch targets that are comfortable for thumb interaction. Important navigation and support actions should remain discoverable without hover.
